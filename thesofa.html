THE SOFA

1941 - 2024

Walnut, leather and linen
94.5 x 33.5 x 37.4 inches (240 x 85 x 95 cm)

The sofa features a sturdy solid beech wood frame, filled with a comfortable spring mattress layered with rubberized horsehair and wool wadding. The base is crafted from rich walnut wood, while the upholstery combines 100% linen fabric with natural vegetable-tanned bull leather.

It is the first numbered edition by Malaparte Design.
